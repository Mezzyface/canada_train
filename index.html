The Transcontinental Winter Trip

Austin ➔ Toronto ➔ The Canadian Rockies ➔ Vancouver

The Game Plan: We're flying into Toronto, hopping aboard VIA Rail's famous restored mid-century streamliner, The Canadian, and taking a 4-day journey across the Canadian Shield, the Prairies, and the Rocky Mountains before ending up in Vancouver.

🤝 The "Buddy System" Savings Addendum

Want to lower that budget? If you pair up and share, here is where the costs drop significantly:

  • Hotels: Splitting a hotel room with a friend immediately cuts the lodging budget in half, saving each person about $315 to $415.
  • The Train: Booking a "Cabin for 2" on the train and splitting it is often cheaper per person than booking individual "Cabin for 1" tickets.

Shared Trip Total: By pairing up, the total cost drops down to roughly $1,615 - $1,980 per person!
