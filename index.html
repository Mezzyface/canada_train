🚂 Train Accommodation Options

VIA Rail's The Canadian offers several accommodation classes. All Sleeper options (Berth, Cabin, and Prestige) include meals in the dining car.

Class Price (USD) Description
Berth $885 Open by day with two couch-style seats facing each other. By night, converts to curtained, semi-private compartments with two stacked beds. Use of public washroom.
Cabin for 1 $1,867 Private room featuring a small couch-style seat that converts into a bed, with a vanity and a concealed toilet.
Cabin for 2 $2,800 Private room with two armchairs, two retractable stacked beds, a vanity and an ensuite toilet.
Prestige $12,080 Experience an unparalleled level of elegance, comfort, and privileged service.

📝 Important Things to Know

What's Included in Your Train Fare

  • Berth, Cabin, and Prestige passengers: All meals are included in the dining car (breakfast, lunch, and dinner)
  • Access to the Skyline Car: Features a stunning dome viewing area on top — perfect for scenic photo opportunities!
  • Showers: Available for all Sleeper Plus and Prestige passengers (communal for cabins/berths, private for Prestige)

Delays Are Common

VIA Rail's passenger trains often experience delays (sometimes hours or even a day late) because freight trains have priority on the tracks. Important: Don't book any connecting flights or activities within 24 hours of your scheduled arrival.

No WiFi on Board

There is no WiFi on the train, and cell service is spotty throughout much of the journey. Download entertainment, books, and offline maps before boarding!

What to Pack

  • Downloaded movies, books, or podcasts for offline entertainment
  • Noise-cancelling headphones
  • Portable charger (power outlets vary by accommodation class)
  • Camera for incredible mountain and prairie views
  • Snacks and drinks (especially important for Berth passengers)
  • Comfortable layers — temperatures can vary on the train

The Most Scenic Section

The stretch between Vancouver and Jasper through the Canadian Rockies is widely considered the most beautiful part of the journey, with snow-capped peaks, turquoise lakes, and dramatic mountain passes.

Discount Opportunities

  • Discount Tuesday: VIA Rail offers weekly deals every Tuesday (use code TUESDAY)
  • Off-season travel: December is considered off-peak, which can mean better prices
  • Group discounts: Available for groups of 6 or more
